# TeamUp MVP Pilot Scope

## Pilot Team

- Internal team (~20 users)
- Roles: Engineering manager, developers, QA, DevOps, designers

## Included Data Scope

- ClickUp workspace: selected during onboarding
- Analysis scope: one selected list/project for MVP
- Historical import: 2-3 months

## Pilot Cadence

- Weekly review meeting (30-45 minutes)
- Agenda:
  1. Top attention tasks
  2. Actions taken by manager
  3. Metric movement (baseline vs current)

## Outcome Criteria

Pilot is considered successful when at least 3 metrics move in the expected direction:

- Median Cycle Time decreases by 10-20%
- Primary bottleneck Time in Status decreases by 15%+
- Rework/Loop rate decreases by 10%+
- Flow Efficiency increases by 5-10 percentage points
- Long-idle task share decreases by 20%+
